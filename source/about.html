I started Sugar&Frogs after moving to Chagrin Falls in 2016 and I couldn't find a decent shirt for my new home. The designs were lacking, they were printed in thick, uncomfortable plastisol, and usually on cheap, boxy cotton tees.

So I set about creating shirts that are better in every way. It starts with high-quality combed, ring-spun cotton and cotton-poly blend tees. All our original designs are screen printed using durable, beautiful water-based inks that actually dye the fabric, so they never crack or peel and after a few washes you can’t even feel it any more.

It doesn’t make any money, but it does allow me to support a bunch of local organizations by doing something I really enjoy. I’ve made or donated shirts and stickers to Safe Routes Chagrin, The Rec, Trike & Bike, PTO, Boosters, and CAA Travel Baseball, and raised money at pop-up shops for After Prom and Boosters.

Please get in touch if you have an organization that could use some help.
